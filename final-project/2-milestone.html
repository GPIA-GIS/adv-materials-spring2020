Final project milestone

In your Final Project Proposal you specified a mid-point milestone for the project. Submit one page where you:

  • Restate the milestone from your proposal;
  • Discuss your progress thus far;
  • Discuss whether you met your milestone or not. If not, discuss why you have not;
  • Include screenshots and links to your in-progress work as appropriate.

Submission

Submit a PDF of your final project milestone. Late submissions will automatically lose 2.5 points (out of 10). Please submit your milestone on time and plan on talking about your proposal for a few minutes in class.
