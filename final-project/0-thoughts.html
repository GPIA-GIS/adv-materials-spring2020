Final project thoughts

Use this casual writing assignment as an opportunity to start thinking about your final project for this course.

Write 1 page about your final project. If you're having a hard time getting started, consider covering any or all of these topics:

  • A project you would like to work on as your final project.

  • Work you have done in the past that you are excited about and that might be a good candidate to publish on the web as an interactive map.

  • Projects that others have done that you might like to emulate.

  • Thoughts about webmaps—what inspires you about them?

  • Map-related skills that you would like to acquire, in this course or at a later date.

There is no right or wrong for this assignment as long as the writing is long enough and is submitted on time. I will take these responses into account while writing future assignments. Your response is the beginning of a discussion about your final project.

In class on Wednesday (February 5) we will casually go around the room and hear people's early ideas, so please be prepared to do so.

Submitting your work

Submit your project thoughts through Canvas. If you do not have access to Canvas, email them to me.
